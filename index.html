Game Instructions

1. The grid consists of lights (white = ON, blue = OFF).

2. Click a cell to toggle it and its adjacent neighbors.

3. Turn all lights ON to win the game.

4. Each game starts with a random solvable configuration.

Good luck, and have fun!

5x5 Lights Out

Click on a cell to toggle it and its neighbors. Turn ON all the lights to win!

Game Tutorial

The goal of Lights Out is to turn ON all the lights on the grid.

Clicking a light toggles its state and the state of its immediate neighbors.

If all lights are turned ON, you win!

Remember: A blue cell means the light is OFF, and a white cell means the light is ON.

Addendum

This game was developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The primary challenge was ensuring the board's starting configuration was solvable while maintaining randomness. The solution involved simulating random clicks on an initially empty board. The grid and styles were designed for clarity and user engagement. This project enhanced my skills in dynamic styling and event handling with JavaScript.
